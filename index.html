PrimeCab Fleet Management Ltd was founded in 2014. Our shareholders have a good reputation and experience for over 20 years' in taxi management as well as taxi service provider in taxi trade. Our mission is to improve the quality of Hong Kong taxi and provide high standard and professional taxi services to passengers. Simultaneously, we provide more qualified, more systematic and more transparency taxi management service to investors and owners in the taxi industry.

PrimeCab Taxi uses Ford's vehicles that have large capacity seating to provide comfortable environment; electric sliding doors for convenient enter and exiting, also with big baggage compartment to accommodate more luggages. In addition, credit card payment system is also available. The fare rate is the same as other taxis with only HK$50 booking fees.
